Creative Writing

I love words! Storytelling is at the heart of everything I do - I love being able to communicate through both prose and poetry (though poetry is my preferred form). I have completed my degree in Literature and Creative Writing with a 2:1 from Keele University, and have a short story published in The Things We Write. I also have had some poems in newsletters, and in the Cold Ashes: A Tabloid of Emotions.

Copywriting

I have had experience writing copy for the Staffordshire-based charity, Make Some Noise! I wrote copy based off interviews that I arranged and conducted with the board of trustees. This was to build up content for the charity to be used for Trustees Week 2024, and for various social media posts throughout the year.

Art

I am an amateur artist who can use both digital and traditional methods! I made a design used by the New Zoo & Adventure Park and a piece used in the McClit newletter!

Short Films

I am an amateur short film maker as well, I have created two short films so far, with Library getting 2nd place at the Keele Reel Film Festival 2024!
